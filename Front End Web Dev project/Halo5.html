The plot of Halo 5: Guardians follows two teams of Spartan soldiers, Blue Team and Fireteam Osiris, as they investigate a new threat to the galaxy. Blue Team is sent to secure a research vessel from the Covenant forces while Fireteam Osiris is deployed to extract Dr. Halsey from Jul 'Mdama's forces. Both teams end up discovering that a rogue artificial intelligence named Cortana has gained access to the Forerunner Domain, a galaxy-spanning repository of Forerunner knowledge. The teams then embark on separate missions to stop Cortana's plan to use the Guardians, ancient and powerful Forerunner machines, to enforce galactic peace by force. As Blue Team follows Cortana to a Forerunner planet, Fireteam Osiris is sent to Sanghelios to seek the help of the Arbiter and retrieve a Constructor Sentinel to access the Domain. The game's story ends with a cliffhanger, setting up a continuation of the conflict in Halo Infinite.
